Product


To reduce your phone usage we have developed an app that will help you achieve this. You can start a challenge with your friends and earn Bubble Points which you can use to do cool things with!



Features


Bubble is an app created to reduce one's screentime. It's way of working with a reward system has proven itself effective on other platforms. You can do it solo or with your friends.

Solo
When playing solo, you can set a goal for your screentime with a maximum of 2,5 hours a day. When staying within this goal you will get 10 bubble points. Every 10 minutes you spend less on your phone with regards to your goal, will give you an extra 5 points. When you cross your goal, Bubble will not lock you out of your phone or apps. However, you will noy receive any points that day even if it's just 5 minutes. Bubble will give you an hour, 30 minutes, 10 minutes, 5 minutes and 1 minute warning. Every 10 minutes you spend longer on your phone than your goal, you will lose 3 points for.

With Friends
Whe playing with your friends, you can create poules. In a poule, you will not need to set a goal. A poule has a minimum of 4 people. The person with the least screentime in a week wins first place. The runner up wins second place and the person following him, her or they will win third place. First place will reward you 15 points, second place will give you 10 points, third place will give you 5 points. Every place following those, will not win or lose any points. However, when a person shreds the limit of 21 hours screentime a week, they will lose 10 points.

Point System
With an amount of 8.000 points you can transfer those points into a 15 euro giftcard for any of our collaborative stores (an overview of these stores is available in the app itself). 14.000 points will get you a 30 euro giftcard.
