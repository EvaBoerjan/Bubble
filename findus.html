The average person uses there mobile device for 3,5 hours a day.

What are the negative side effects of too much screen time?

According to professionals it is physical strain to your eyes and body. Spending long hours staring at a screen definitely takes a toll on your body, especially your eyes. Excessive screen time not only strains your eyes and leaves them feeling dry, but can also lead to retina damage and blurred vision.Furthermore, being constantly hunched over (like how so many people tend to do with their smartphones) also affects your posture and can cause stiffness and pain both the neck and shoulder.
