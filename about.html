About


Our vision

Bubble is a brand that stands for friendship, unity, connection, and simplicity. We live in a world where everyone uses their phone more than ever, with major consequences that lead to less and less social contact. You've probably seen a couple eating in a restaurant where both people spend the whole evening on their phones and don't actually make contact with each other. Bubble offers the perfect solution and ensures that these situations are a thing of the past!

What do we want to achieve

We want to make real connection happen again. When you are with your friends you should enjoy all the time you have together, not on your phones. This app will help you and the company you find yoursel with to truly connect. The app will track your screen time with an exciting twist. The less you go on your phone, the more points you get. These points translate into money. Being able to compete against your friends for the least screentime adds another layer of fun!
